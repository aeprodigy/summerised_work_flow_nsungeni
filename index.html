Nsungeni Children's Home

Nsungeni Children’s Home is a non-Governmental Child Welfare Organization whose objectives are to provide long and short-term solutions to the children in need of child protection facilities regardless of gender, colour and religious affiliation. What we want for our children

Our Mission statement

Our mission is to come to the rescue of the orphans
and vulnerable children from social isolation and has access
to all their basic needs, including but not limited to, education,
health, shelter, food and clothing.

Our Vision

Our vision is to facilitate a society where children
are given equal opportunities to live a sustainable
and peaceful life that affirm their self-worth and dignity.

Our Core Values

Social justice and Equity

We will work with other stakeholders to promote equality,
dignity and more just society, especially with regard to the poor and
marginalized.

Gender and Diversity

Our work will reflect and promote respect for gender and
diversity issues.

Accountability

We are accountable and responsible to our actions and
uphold ethical behavior and responsibility.

Our Values

These are the core beliefs and attitudes on which our organisation has been built, and they are the cornerstones of our success. These values guide our actions, decisions and relationships as we work towards fulfilling our mission.

Integrity

We are committed to always follow ethical principles on child welfare to improve the lives of needy children.

Loyalty

Furthermore we are committed to being consistent and devoted to improving the lives of needy children.

TrustWorthiness

Being trustworthy is one of our key values, to ensure that we manage funds entrusted to us and channel them solely for the well-being of the children.

Inspiration

It is our commitment to inspire the children at Nsungeni CCF to become active and prosperous.
